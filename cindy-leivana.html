Cindy Leivana

About Cindy Leivana

Cindy Leivana, based in Yogyakarta, has achieved notable success in the field of vocal performance. She captured the 2nd place in the prestigious National Student Arts Festival (FLS2N), showcasing her exceptional talent and dedication to her craft. Additionally, Cindy's journey includes being a finalist in the highly competitive La Academia Junior 2 competition aired on SCTV. Performing with her band called The Fairies, Cindy predominantly utilizes orchestration in her songs, adding depth and richness to her performances. These achievements underscore her musical prowess and highlight her established presence in the music industry. Cindy's remarkable accomplishments serve as a testament to her passion for music and her relentless pursuit of excellence in her art.
